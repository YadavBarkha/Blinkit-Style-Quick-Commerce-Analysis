## 🔍 Project Overview

This project analyzes quick commerce (Blinkit-style) data to understand product pricing, discount strategies, customer demand, and regional performance. The objective is to derive actionable business insights using interactive Tableau dashboards.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Quick commerce platforms operate on tight margins and high competition. This project aims to answer:

* How do discounts impact customer demand?
* Which product categories perform best?
* How does pricing vary across cities?
* What drives higher sales and availability?

---

## 📁 Dataset Description

The dataset includes product-level and transactional information:

* **Product Name** – Item available on the platform
* **Category** – Product category (Fruits, Dairy, Snacks, Beverages, etc.)
* **MRP** – Maximum Retail Price
* **Selling Price** – Discounted selling price
* **Discount (%)** – Calculated discount percentage
* **City** – Location (Mumbai, Pune, Nagpur, etc.)
* **Availability** – In Stock / Out of Stock
* **Rating** – Customer rating
* **Orders** – Number of orders per product

---

## 📊 Key Visualizations (Worksheets)

### 1. Total Products (KPI)

Displays total number of products listed.

### 2. Total Orders (KPI)

Shows overall demand across the platform.

### 3. Average Discount (KPI)

Represents average discount percentage offered.

### 4. Category-wise Sales

Compares order volume across categories.

### 5. Price Comparison (MRP vs Selling Price)

Analyzes pricing differences and discount impact.

### 6. Top Discounted Products

Highlights products offering the highest discounts.

### 7. City-wise Orders

Shows regional demand distribution.

### 8. Availability Analysis

Visualizes in-stock vs out-of-stock products.

## 📖 Storytelling Insights

### 📌 Story 1: Pricing & Demand Relationship

* Products with higher discounts tend to generate more orders
* Certain categories consistently offer higher discounts

👉 **Conclusion:** Discounts significantly influence customer purchasing behavior.

---

### 📌 Story 2: Regional Performance Analysis

* Demand varies across cities
* Some regions show higher product availability and sales

👉 **Conclusion:** Location plays a key role in pricing strategy and inventory planning.

## 📊 Key Insights

* High-discount products drive higher sales
* Categories like snacks and beverages dominate demand
* Regional differences affect both pricing and availability
